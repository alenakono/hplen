Properties and features of NPL panels

Protective layer (overlay)

A special film of high-strength hardened melamine resin. It is on it comes the main loads. Protects the material from:

  • damage
  • chemicals
  • UV rays
  • mold and mildew

Layer-decor

High-quality paper that determines the final appearance of the laminate. More than 250 different decors allow you to implement the most daring and creative design ideas.

Protective decorative layer (anderlay)

A special paper that enhances the color reproduction of the decor. Performs additional protection against mixing.

Basic layer

It consists of sheets of Kraft paper pressed together by a special technology. The number of sheets determines the thickness of the final product (from 0.6 mm to 25 mm). Depending on the customer's wishes, components are added to make the material incombustible and postforming.
